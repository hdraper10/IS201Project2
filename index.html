Saint George

The Sun Town of Utah

Top Reasons to visit Saint George

  1. The weather is amazing and the sun is almost always shining.
  2. The city is central to some of the most stunning natural beauties in America.
  3. The city is close to several State and National Parks.
  4. Although nature is nearby there are plenty of restaurants and stores nearby.
  5. Saint George has many historical sites that contribute to its rich culture and tradition. These sites are accesible to all visitors.

Fun Facts

  • Named in honor of an apostle of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints: George A. Smith.
  • It is the fifth-largest city in Utah.
  • Saint George is a hotspot for golfers and hikers from around the western United States.
  • The city was settled in 1861 as part of a cotton mission which earned it the nickname Dixie.
